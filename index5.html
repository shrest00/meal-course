Easter Course Meal

A Delicious Holiday Menu

Starter: Fresh Salad

Mixed greens, tomatoes, and vinaigrette. Toss ingredients and serve chilled.

Soup: Creamy Vegetable Soup

Blend cooked vegetables, add cream, simmer for 10 minutes and season.

Side: Garlic Bread

Spread butter and garlic on bread. Bake until golden brown.

Main Course: Roast Chicken

Season chicken with herbs and roast in oven for 45 minutes.

Dessert: Chocolate Cake

Mix ingredients, bake for 30 minutes, and add chocolate frosting.

Drink: Fresh Juice

Blend oranges, strawberries, and ice. Serve chilled.
